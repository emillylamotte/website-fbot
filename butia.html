FBOT - BUTIABOTS

ABOUT THE TEAM

Since 2018, a partnership established by Taura Bots from Federal University of Santa Maria (UFSM) and FBOT from Federal University of Rio Grande (FURG), started the Brazilian United Team for Intelligent Automation - BUTIA. The Butia Bots team already has several participations in RoboCup@Home, being the third best team for 3 consecutive years and the current vice champion of the CBR/LARC 2021!
